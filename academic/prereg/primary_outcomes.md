# Academic Preregistration: Primary Outcomes

## Primary Outcomes

1. **ΔEnergy_post − ΔEnergy_pre** (0–100 scale)
   - Measured within 24 hours of intervention
   - Self-reported on 0–100 scale

2. **HRV RMSSD change** within 24 h post-intervention
   - Measured via ECG or wearable device
   - Change from baseline (pre-intervention) to 24h post

## Evidence Threshold

- **Bayes Factor BF10 ≥ 10** (default), OR
- **Frequentist p ≤ 0.005** with preregistered model

AND a corresponding standardized effect size with 95% CI must be reported.

# Memetic Experiment Consent Language

## Purpose

You are contributing to a public, non-therapeutic citizen-science study on attention and energy. Participation is voluntary.

## What We Collect

- De-identified self-report scores (0–100) for energy before and after a deletion event
- Optional: Wearable summaries (e.g., HRV RMSSD, sleep efficiency) if you choose to share
- Basic demographics: age band, city (free text)
- Timestamps of deletion events and consent

## Medical Disclaimer

**No medical advice is provided.** Do not change medications or health routines without consulting a clinician.

This is a self-reflective experiment, not therapy or treatment. If you experience distress, please seek professional support.

## Data Rights

- You may request deletion of your raw record at any time using your Participant ID
- Aggregated statistics may still be retained even if your individual record is removed
- De-identified, aggregated results will be publicly released
